How we built this

The data behind the ranking

  • Ranking model trained on 1.5 million real job applications — actual hire and no-hire outcomes, not synthetic data
  • 5 million resume database built over 10+ years of Zippia job search platform operation
  • Also trained on 100,000+ AI-optimized resumes — ranking is resistant to keyword inflation and AI-generated resume gaming
  • Evaluates career trajectory and inferred skills, not just keyword overlap with the job description
  • Explainable scoring: every rank includes visible reasoning you can audit, document, and show your legal team
  • You make the decisions. The ranking is a tool, not a gatekeeper. You review and act.

Current limitations

  • Optimized for professional and knowledge-worker roles; effectiveness may vary for highly specialized or executive positions
  • Not a legal compliance product — you remain responsible for your hiring decisions and documentation
  • Does not do outbound sourcing — ranks inbound applicants you've already received
